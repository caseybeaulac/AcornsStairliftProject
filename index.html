Why Choose Acorn Stairlifts?

Since our earliest days in 1992, Acorn has strived to set new standards in quality and innovation for the stairlift industry. Our pioneering designs and engineering excellence have led the way and helped Acorn grow to become the world's leading independent company specialising solely in the manufacture and installation of stairlifts. Our head office is located in Florida with a network of stairlift surveyors and engineers all over the USA. Over 20 years experience and knowledge means that we are America's leading stairlift manufacturer.

Cutting Edge

Built to exacting standards at our factories, our cutting edge stairlifts are now exported to more than 80 countries worldwide. By the time you have read through our website, we will have given someone else's life a lift — that’s because an Acorn Stairlift is installed somewhere in the world every eight minutes, 365 days-a-year.

Unwavering Customer Focus

Acorn remains a down-to-earth business that has never lost sight of the most important asset at its very core — our customers.

Unique

We appreciate that everyone is different and unique, with their own reasons for wanting or needing one of our stairlifts. From the moment we answer your first phone call to us, our job is to listen and understand your needs. Your free home survey will give us a clearer picture, and only then will we be able to recommend an Acorn Stairlift which will best meet your needs and enhance your quality of life.

Customer feedback

We don't stop listening once your stairlift has been installed. Our comprehensive servicing and aftercare package ensures we benefit from continual customer feedback, helping to inform and guide future development and refinement of our stairlifts.

Engineering excellence

With millions of pounds worth of investment in our manufacturing facilities, we are ideally placed to continue delivering Acorn Stairlifts directly to our customers. Attention to detail and engineering excellence mean you can be assured of a top-class stairlift which will serve you for years to come.
